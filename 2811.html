Grand Prize! Shenzhen Environmental Water Group's smart water construction has won another honor!

2024-01-03

Recently

The Second "I+Innovation Cup" Industrial Internet Innovation Competition

Successfully concluded

Subordinated by Shenzhen Environmental Water Group

Shenzhen Deepwater Bao'an Water Group, Shenzhen Water Technology Company

Joint declaration

Shenzhen Bao'an Smart Water Construction Project

After multiple rounds of selection and intense competition

Finally won the National Special Award




The project is characterized by outstanding innovation and practicality

Received high praise from the judges



The project focuses on empowering tap water for direct drinking, and innovatively develops and applies multiple high-end digital technologies to promote the digital transformation and innovative development of the water industry. It successfully creates a benchmark for smart water management and has a wide impact in the industry.


1

Realize full process intelligence in water plant production: By constructing intensive smart water plants, achieve intelligent control of the entire production chain, significantly enhance the water quality assurance capability of water plants, and improve their production efficiency.


2

Pioneering adaptive mode for water supply scheduling: Overcoming 8 core technologies and over 40 technical challenges, pioneering an adaptive water supply scheduling mode that enables real-time scheduling, second level calculation, intelligent optimization, and precise pressure control without human intervention.


3

Digital direct drinking safeguards drinking water safety: creating a digital direct drinking control platform to achieve comprehensive control of all regions, all periods, all scenarios, all elements, and all processes, ensuring the safety and reliability of water supply.


4

User profiling enhances service quality: deeply insight into needs, provide personalized services, and improve customer service experience.



△ Smart Water Plant Architecture


The project construction achievements have not only been successfully applied in 9 water treatment plants in Bao'an District, Shenzhen, but also widely applied in more than 40 projects in provinces such as Guangdong, Shaanxi, and Zhejiang, all of which have achieved significant results, fully demonstrating the reliability and wide application prospects of the project achievements.

△ Five Finger Rake Smart Water Plant


By implementing the results of the smart water management construction project, Bao'an District's water management construction has achieved a dual improvement in economic and social benefits. In terms of economic benefits, the construction of smart water management significantly improves water supply efficiency, reduces operating costs, and reduces energy consumption; In terms of social benefits, the successful implementation of this project has provided safe and reliable high-quality water supply services to 5.5 million residents in Bao'an District.
