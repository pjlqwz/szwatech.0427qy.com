01

三款新品 首次亮相

2024.4.18-20



The biggest highlight of this year's China Water Association Annual Meeting is the three newly launched products brought by Shenzhen Water Science and Technology Company:


✔ There is a water plant intelligent dosing integrated machine equipped with edge hardware control system, high-performance computing processing capability, real-time calculation, and intelligent algorithm model for pharmaceuticals.


✔ There is a water tank cleaning robot suitable for reservoir environments, which relies on autonomous navigation and remote control to sense the water level and pollution level of the pool and clean the bottom and walls of the pool.


✔ There is also an intelligent aeration system that can save energy and reduce consumption, and can be widely used in mainstream activated sludge treatment processes. It can calculate the real-time air volume of the biochemical tank, control the required air volume, and adjust the valve opening.
