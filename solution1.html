Smart Raw Water Early-Warning & Management Solution
Integrated data from reservoirs, pumping stations, and pipeline networks to build an early warning system that monitors water quality, conditions, meteorology, and industrial status. Combined with the Smart Raw Water Early Warning and Control Platform, this enables real-time detection of raw water changes and early warnings for sudden pollution risks, enhancing urban water supply resilience through networked scheduling.
  • Overview of Entire Situation
  • Scientific Early Warning
  • Predetermined Plan
  • Event Rehearsal
  • Emergency Linkage
  • Overview of Entire Situation
    BIM and digital twin technology display multiple water source information on a single screen, integrating real-time monitoring data to help managers fully grasp the current raw water status.
  • Scientific Early Warning
    Build a "multi-place integrated" ubiquitous IoT perception system, form an early warning network for water quality and water quantity in water source, regularly monitor water quality and algae indicators in water source, and give early warning of risks such as raw water mutation and algae outbreak in advance.
  • Predetermined Plan
    Combined with the customer's local historical and seasonal data, build their own data and model database, predict the water situation changes, and set up water quality risk emergency plans in advance.
  • Event Rehearsal
    Comprehensive hydro-meteorological and other multidimensional data, the simulated drill supervision area pollution, water situation, flood situation, intrusion and other events, to predict the impact range and risk degree of the events.
  • Emergency Linkage
    According to the water source location and algorithm model, the water source allocation plan is generated in multiple source. Realize unified supervision and intelligent linkage dispatching of multiple water sources, ensure raw water supply, and effectively reduce the impact of water quality changes on reservoir water intake.
