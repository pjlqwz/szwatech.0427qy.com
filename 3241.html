High-Frequency Pressure Monitor
This device is capable of real-time monitoring of pipeline wall pressure, record pressure anomalies locations, and integrating valve action data to locate and analyse water hammer phenomenon. Concurrently, this device can utilize foundational information such as pipeline structure, material, and diameter to assist in analyzing the operational status of the pipeline, thereby effectively reducing the risk of pipeline bursts.
Product Features
24-bit acquisition chip for high-precision sampling
Damping buffer design to reduce sensor impact
Accurate water hammer monitoring to reduce burst pipe risks
AI algorithm to eliminate interference and accurately identify leaks
