Water Supply Full-Process Scheduling Solution
Integrate real-time monitoring data from water sources, water plants, pumping stations, pipe networks, and quality checkpoints to supervise water supply factors via the Water Supply Full-Process Management Platform. This platform accurately predicts water supply with a ±2% deviation, guiding schedules and reducing production-marketing discrepancies. By coordinating production, pipe network, fieldwork orders, and customer service subsystems, as well as closing the emergency management loop, water supply service quality is further improved.
Key Features of the Solution
Full-Process HACCP Management System
Drawing on extensive experience in water quality management and leveraging the expertise of experts, we have developed the 'HACCP Food-Grade Water Quality Management System' to facilitate intelligent oversight of the entire water supply process. This system encompasses risk identification, early warning, and responsive measures to ensure timely prediction, detection, and mitigation of water quality risks.
Lab Scientization Management
Use "Laboratory Management System" to standardize water quality testing work.
Intelligent Scheduling To Reduce Energy Consumption
Simulate and analyze the pressure distribution, water age, and water supply boundaries of the network, and combine the scheduling model with real-time monitoring data to provide the best scheduling settings.
Leakage Management System
By employing DMA management, minimum nighttime flow, and hydraulic balance analysis alongside mathematical models, we can identify network leaks through multi-point, multi-indicator analysis, thereby reducing leakage rates and enhancing water supply efficiency and quality.
