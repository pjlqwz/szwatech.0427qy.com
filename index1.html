About Us
A state-owned technology enterprise
under Shenzhen Water and
Environment Group

Established in 1998, Shenzhen Water Technologies (hereinafter referred to as “we”) develop with Shenzhen Special Economic Zone and Shenzhen Water and Environment Group, focusing on automation, informatization, and intelligence. With a profound understanding of the water industry, we have a thorough independent research and development system, holding nearly 200 patents and software copyrights. A comprehensive system encompassing ‘Smart Water-Related Affairs Solution, Software Systems And Platforms, Hardware And Software Integrated Products, and Services relevant to Water Affairs’ is set up to meet diverse market demands with IoT, Cloud Computing, Big Data, AI integrated. We could support you to realize integrated and intelligent management from source water to tap water, from sewage treatment to basin environmental governance.

Our benchmarked exemplary projects have been repeatedly shortlisted as typical cases of smart water utility by the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, as well as honors awarded by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology in China, ranking at the forefront of domestic smart water management concepts and applications
