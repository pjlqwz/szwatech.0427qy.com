Energy-Saving & Consumption-Reducing Controller For Secondary Water Supply
A self-developed product that focuses on energy saving and consumption reduction for secondary pressure water supply facilities, collect and calculate data from the most unfavorable point in the secondary water supply system. This product intelligently analyzes the trend of changes in user demand and reduces electricity consumption through the self-adaptive adjustment algorithm.

Intelligent Dosing System for Water Plants
In terms of water quality indicators such as turbidity, pH, disinfection and odor, the product equipped with a variety of pharmaceutical intelligent dosing model, integrates PLC automatic control, water quantity and quality monitor, and floc alum observation system. Via the ‘feed forward + model + feedback’ mode, the unattended dosage of a variety of chemicals in the water plant is achieved, accurate dosage to save chemical consumption, and ensure the stability of water quality and reach the standards constantly, aiming to improve the level of water plant intelligence.
