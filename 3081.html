Urban Drainage & Flood Prevention Solution
Based on the topology of "Plant - Station - Pipeline Network - Household" and online monitoring data, this solution can comprehensively supervise urban drainage and flood prevention facilities, waterlogging spots, and drainage pipeline (channels) while integrated application of GIS, BIM, hydraulic model, etc., to provide real-time supervision, operation and maintenance coordination, traceability analysis, intelligent scheduling, preview and early warning. Help customers improve urban drainage facilities' management and scheduling capabilities to reduce the urban waterlogging risk.
  • Emergency response time: 2 minutes
  • Manual intervention frequency: 0.3 times/hour
  • Tracing Pollution in Seconds
  • Rainfall Prevention Capability: 75 mm per hour (Rainfall Intensity)
Technology Empowers Flood Prevention & Drainage in Shenzhen
Through the construction of the "Sky-ground-water" ubiquitous supervision system, all business-related data, such as water quality and water level, of the entire drainage process from the municipal rainwater pipe network to the terminal river facilities and equipment are mastered in real-time.

Managing 9046 Drainage Communities

Managing: over 20000 km drainage pipeline network

Managing: 850000 drainage inspection Wells

Managing: nearly 600000 Rainwater outfall
